Target Audience

The audience for the Chamber of Commerce Site is anyone who is looking for more information about Allen Texas. More specifically the site is targeted towards people who own local small businesses in Allen and want their businesses to be advertised with residents. The site will also help local Allen residents find the businesses they are looking for.

Personas

Frank: Local business owner
Owns a bike shop.
Is a family man.
Franks Goal
To get more people to use and recognize his business.

Mary: A local school teacher
Mary works at the local elemetry school.
Marys Goal
Mary wants to use the Allen Chamber of Commerce site to find local workers and business men to come and talk to her class.

Scenarios

Different scenarios for why people could be going to the Allen chamber of commerse site couold include many different things. Some of those reasons are as follows.
- Looking for a spesifec business.
- Trying to sell your product to local businesses.
- Looking for fun things to do in Allen.
- Looking for discounts and sales going on.
