Target Audience

The audience for the Chamber of Commerce Site is anyone who is looking for more information about Allen Texas. More specifically the site is targeted towards people who own local small businesses in Allen and want their businesses to be advertised with residents. The site will also help local Allen residents find the businesses they are looking for.
