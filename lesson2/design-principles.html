White Space and Clean Design

ETQ

The ETQ website sells shoes and their website is extremely clean and uses white space to its advantage.
On the home page as you see captured in the image above you can see a snippet of the clan design they have used.
One thing that really makes a difference in this site is that they went the extra mile to make not only the site and design clean but also all the images on the site are very clean looking, match each other, and use white space.

PARC: Proximity

JULUANA

Good use of proximity is really important in a website and the Juluana site has good use of proximity.
You can't tell as much from this one screenshot but as you scroll through this bike site everything is spaced out perfectly and all the elements and titles that go with each other are spaced in such a way that your mind automatically know they go together.

Fitt's Law

IMPOSIBLE BUREAU

The Imposible Bureau site is a great example of Fit's Law because it's full of really interactive designs and features.
The loading screen alone has a really cool effect where the colors change when the mouse moves, and when you go to the other pages in the site there are more effects that draw the viewer in and keep them interested.
