Missionary Services

Senior service missionaries may now be assigned to serve in temples in the United States and Canada when temples move to phase 4 operations. They may serve in the administrative office, youth center, cafeteria, clothing, laundry, custodial, facilities maintenance, grounds, and in security.

Volunteers serving in temple baptistries and ordinance-support areas (such as the recommend desk and sealing office) will continue to be called by the temple president through the standard recommendation process. All other long-term volunteer positions will become service missionary assignments.

If you are interested in learning more please contact the in at: dallastempleinn@lds.org
